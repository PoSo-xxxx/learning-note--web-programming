style(css)

define the appearence of a tag, using css format.

CSS

CSS:(Cascading Style Sheet) is a format for ddefining the apearence of a tag in html.
format:selector:{attribute:value;}
  • seletor:a tag, class or id, which kind of block to define.
  • the declartion:
    • tag->tag
    • class->.class
    • id->#id
    • tag with class->tag.class
    • tag with id->tag#id

element of tag in css:

  • for character style:

    • text-decoration:none | underline | overline | line-through
    • text-transform:none | capitalize | uppercase | lowercase
    • color: color's name | rgb(r, g, b) | #XXXXXX(hexadecimal color code)
    • font-family: font's name
    • font-weight:100~900 | normal(400) | bold(700) | bolder | lighter
    • font-style:normal | italic | oblique
    • font-size:
      • absolute size:xx-small | x-small | small | medium | large | x-large | xx-large
      • relative size:larger smaller
      • length:in | cm | mm | pt
        | pc | em | ex | px
      • proportion:em | %

  • border:

    • border: border-width | border-style | color
    • border-width:
      • thin | medium | thick
      • unit:pt | in | cm | px
    • border-style:none | solid | double | dotted | dashed | groove | ridge | inset | outset
    • color: color's name | rgb(r, g, b) | #XXXXXX(hexadecimal color code)

  • a(hyperlink):

    • link:define the style of hyperlink.
    • visited:define the style of visited hyperlink.
    • hover:define the style when mouse move over the hyperlink.
    • active:define the style of a seleceted hyperlink.

  • list:

    • list-style-type:none | disc | circle | square | decimal | decimal-leadinf-zero | lower-alpha | upper-alpha | upper-roman
    • list-style-image:url
    • list-style-position:inside | outside

float&clear:

the special element in css.

  • float:none | left | right

    the block which have set the float, it will float next to the closest block near it.

  • cleat:none | left | right

    cancel the float effect to a block which near the floating block.
